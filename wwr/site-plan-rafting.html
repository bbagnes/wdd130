Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to reach out to the younger generation; especially those who explore their world almost entirely through online sites and services; and bring them to know the joy of exploring the physical world through white water rafting. Also, to expand the business by reaching out to potential explorers from various nations around the world who might be interested in experiencing the joy of white water rafting.

Audience

This website is to intended to target the younger generation whose primary source of entertainment and education is the internet; especially to increase bookings by youth groups and school groups. Also, to expand the customer base among sinlge adult groups of all ages, newly weds, and younger families by promoting an experience that is exciting, scenic, recreational, and educational. °What do they want? Youths and single adults of all ages are looking for exciting activities that can boast about to their friends and peers, as well as a social environment where they can meet other people their own age. Newly weds are looking for a unique and exciting experience for their honeymoon that is within their budget. Families are looking for a fun experience that is healthy, recreational, and holds interest and potential educational and cultural experiences for all of the members of their family. °What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The website will provide information on a variety of rafting explorations fit to the seekers schedule and budget. It will also demonstrate the fun recreational and scenic value of white water rafting, and a hint of the educational and cultural offerings available for experience to fit young families and youth groups. °How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Younger explorers will probably access the site using cell phones, or other portable viewing devices. Older single adults, and heads of families may also view the site using cell phones, but also through desktop or laptop devices. Ensuring a site that is accessable on all types of devices will be beneficial, even necessary.
