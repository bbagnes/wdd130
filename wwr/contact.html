Meet Our Staff

As our Resident Historian, David prides himself on his extensive knowledge of the history and cultures of the First Nation tribes that resided along all of our guided tours. If ever you have a quesiton about anything history related to our tours, he is the man to go to. But be warned: he is liable to talk your ears off; if you let him.

Heralding all the way from Perth, Australia; Sheilia grew up exploring the wilds surrounding her hometown before immigrating the the US in 2010. An expert on plants and wildlife, she offers her knowledge of traditional medicines and folk rememdies to anyone who asks. As our financial specialist, she is also the go-to for group package and repeat business discounts.

Having started white water rafting at the age of twelve, Cedric is a veteran with over twenty years, and two hundred tours of experience. He has seen almost everything in his time with Explorers', and always has an answer for any situation. For that reason Cedric is our go-to guy for all things related to white water rafting and wilderness survival.
