Explorers' White Water Adventures

More Than Just Rafting

At Explorers' White Water Adventures we have a passion for the rich history and cultures of the regions we lead our fellow adventurers through. For this reason every adventure we offer is accompanied by a native guide who is well versed in the history and customs of the indigenous people; some of whom were their own ancestors. A portion of the time on every river adventure is also specifically set aside so that our fellow adventurers can explore the various historic sites along these rivers, take photographs of them, and emerse themselves in the engaging history and cultures that lived along and created them; should they desire to do so.
